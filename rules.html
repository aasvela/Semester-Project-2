Rules

  • 1. No bidding on your own items: Bidding on your own items is strictly prohibited. This includes bidding through a third party or using fake accounts. Any member found to be bidding on their own items will be permanently banned from the website.
  • 2. Age restriction: To participate in our whisky auctions, you must be at least 20 years old. We take age verification seriously and may request proof of age before allowing participation in our auctions.
  • 3. No blended whisky: We are a website dedicated to the finest and rarest whiskies, and as such, we do not allow the sale or auctioning of blended whiskies. Any member found to be selling or auctioning blended whiskies will receive a lifetime ban from the website.
  • 4. Payment and shipping: Payment must be made within three days of winning an auction, and shipping must be completed within seven days. Failure to comply with these rules may result in a temporary suspension of your account.
  • 5. Whisky condition: All whisky bottles must be in good condition. Any bottles found to be damaged or otherwise unfit for consumption will be removed from the auction and the seller will be banned from the website.
